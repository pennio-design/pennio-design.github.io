The 30-Day Process

Week 1 — Diagnose Confusion

Identify exactly what’s unclear and why it’s costing you sales. We analyze who you're really selling to and where pricing resistance comes from.

Outcome: Clear understanding of the problem

Week 2 — Define Your Position

We define your best-fit customer, the real alternative you compete against, and the one thing you do better than anyone else.

Outcome: A positioning statement you can actually use

Week 3 — Clarify Pricing & Messaging

We build simple pricing logic and plain-language messaging that explains your value.

Outcome: You stop rambling, prospects stop hesitating

Week 4 — Turn Clarity into a Sales Asset

We create one core sales asset (page, deck, or one-pager) and reusable language for all communications.

Outcome: Selling stops depending on your mood
